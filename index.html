Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter

The Legend

  • September 4, 1981: Beyoncé Giselle Knowles was born in Houston, Texas.
  • November 11, 1997: Destiny's Child releases their first hit single "No, No, No".
  • February 17, 1998: Destiny's Child releases self-titled debut album.
  • February 29, 2000: Destiny's Child Release "Say My Name" Single in the US.
  • May 20, 2001: Destiny's Child releases "Bootylicious" single.
  • June 11, 2002: Beyoncé Knowles releases first solo single "Work It Out".
  • June 22, 2003: Beyoncé releases her debut album "Dangerously in Love".
  • November 10, 2004: Destiny's Child releases "Destiny Fulfilled".
  • July 19, 2005: Destiny's Child releases "Cater 2 U" single.
  • September 5, 2006: Beyoncé releases second studio album "B'Day".
  • April 10 2007 to Dec 31 2007: The Beyoncé Experience second concert tour.
  • October 8, 2008: Beyoncé releases "Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It)" single.
  • January 20, 2009: Beyoncé releases "Diva" single.
  • January 31, 2010: Beyoncé wins six out of ten nominations - including Song of the Year for "Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It) " - giving her the most wins in one night of any female artist.
  • August 28, 2011: Beyoncé reveals her pregnancy during the MTV Video Music Awards.
  • January 7, 2012: Beyoncé and Jay-Z have their first child: a daughter named Blue Ivy Carter.
  • August 6, 2012: Forbes declares Beyoncé and Jay-Z the highest-paid celebrity couple, with earnings of $78 million.
  • February 3, 2013: Beyoncé invigorates the 2013 Super Bowl halftime show with a performance that reunites Destiny's Child.
  • June 25, 2014:Beyoncé and Jay-Z kick off their On The Run tour with a show in Miami, with the couple co-headlining.
  • April 23, 2016. Beyoncé releases Lemonade album with no advanced warning.
  • February 1, 2017: In a post on Instagram that is liked over 8 million times in 24 hours, Beyoncé reveals she is pregnant with twins.
  • April 14, 2018: Beyoncé played the first of two weekends as the headlining act of the Coachella Music Festival.
  • April 17, 2019: Beyoncé alongside media-service provider Netflix, premiered the 137-minute documentary and concert film Homecoming, mainly regarding Beyoncé's historic 2018 Coachella performances.
